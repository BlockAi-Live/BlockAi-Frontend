# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with BlockAI in under 2 minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit [blockai.live/signup](https://blockai.live/signup) and choose your preferred sign-up method:

- **Wallet Connect** (recommended) — Click "Connect Wallet" and approve the connection in MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Rainbow, or Rabby
- **OAuth** — Sign in with Google, GitHub, or Twitter
- **Email** — Enter your full name, email, and password

> 💡 You'll receive **20 free AI credits** immediately — no credit card required.

## Step 2: Explore Your Dashboard

After signing in, you'll land on the **Dashboard** — your command center:

- **Wallet Balance** — Connect a wallet to see your ETH balance and USD equivalent
- **AI Credits** — Your remaining credit balance
- **AI News** — Live crypto news powered by ChainGPT
- **Daily Rewards** — Claim free points every day (7-day streak system)
- **Watchlist** — Top coins with live sparkline charts

## Step 3: Try Your First AI Chat

1. Click **"New Chat"** on the dashboard (or navigate to `/chat`)
2. Select your AI model — **ChainGPT** (crypto-native) or **BlockAI 3.0** (Gemini-powered)
3. Ask anything:
   - _"What's the current state of Ethereum L2s?"_
   - _"Explain impermanent loss in simple terms"_
   - _"What are the top DeFi protocols by TVL?"_

Each message costs **1 credit**.

## Step 4: Scan a Wallet

1. Navigate to **Chain Scanner** from the sidebar
2. Paste any Ethereum wallet address
3. BlockAI will fetch:
   - Full transaction history from Etherscan
   - Token balances and transfers
   - AI-generated risk assessment and activity report

## Step 5: Generate a Smart Contract

1. Navigate to **Smart Contracts** from the sidebar
2. Switch to **"Generate"** mode
3. Describe what you want:
   - _"Create an ERC-20 token called BlockCoin with 1 million supply and a 2% burn mechanism"_
4. BlockAI generates production-ready Solidity code instantly
