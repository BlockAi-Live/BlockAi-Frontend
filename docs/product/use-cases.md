# Use Cases

## For Retail Traders

### Daily Market Intelligence

Open BlockAI each morning to check your portfolio, read AI-curated news, and claim daily rewards. Use Market Analysis to run AI reports on any coin before making trading decisions.

### Whale Tracking

Paste a whale wallet address into Chain Scanner to see their full transaction history, token holdings, and AI-generated behavior analysis. Set Smart Alerts to get notified when they make large moves.

### Alpha Discovery

Use AI Signals to discover market trends, then ask the AI Chat for deeper analysis on specific tokens or protocols.

---

## For Web3 Developers

### Rapid Prototyping

Describe your smart contract in plain English — "Create a staking contract with 7-day lock period and proportional rewards" — and get production-ready Solidity code in seconds.

### Automated Security

Before deploying, paste your contract code into the Auditor for an AI-driven security review covering reentrancy, access control, integer issues, and gas optimization.

### Research & Reference

Use AI Chat to ask technical questions about Solidity patterns, ERC standards, DeFi mechanics, or gas optimization strategies.

---

## For NFT Creators

### Art Generation

Use the NFT Generator to create artwork from text descriptions. Iterate on prompts until you get the perfect piece, then download for minting.

### Collection Planning

Use AI Chat to brainstorm collection themes, rarity systems, and smart contract requirements for your NFT project.

---

## For DeFi Power Users

### Transaction Forensics

Decode complex multi-hop DeFi transactions using the Transaction Decoder. Understand exactly what happened — which tokens moved, to where, and why.

### Portfolio Monitoring

Connect your wallet to track balances across tokens. Set alerts for significant price movements or unusual activity on watched wallets.

### Gas Optimization

Configure gas alerts to notify you when network fees drop below your threshold, so you can execute transactions at optimal times.
